About Me

I am studying Information and Communication Technology at LAB University of Applied Sciences. My main interests are applied machine learning, data analysis, computer vision, backend development, and system integration.

I have worked with Python, JavaScript, C, C++, Go, Swift, Git, Docker, Flask, Express, SQL, MongoDB, OpenCV, and YOLO. My experience includes backend development for computer vision systems, real-time video processing, object detection, REST APIs for frontend integration, and small software projects built during coursework and personal learning.

I am also comfortable working on frontend when needed. I prefer clean, lightweight, and minimal interfaces that are easy to use and do not overload the user.

This portfolio mainly contains older frontend and data visualization learning projects built with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and vanilla JavaScript. My newer and more technical projects are partly private or will be presented separately on GitHub.
